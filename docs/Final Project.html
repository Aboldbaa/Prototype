Question 1

Which organizational characteristics most strongly predict whether a nonprofit is high-revenue?

To determine how factors such as mission type (NTEE major group), organizational structure (group-affiliated vs standalone), geography (state), financial attributes (assets, revenue), and fiscal year-end month contribute to predicting whether a nonprofit has income above the median for the year 2024.

  HighIncome ~ NTEE_major + GroupStatus + STATE + ACCT_PD,
